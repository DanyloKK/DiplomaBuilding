Electrical installation

Residential

A home's electrical system connects to the utility company's power lines at the service entrance, it usually consists of a weatherhead, a meter socket, and a main circuit breaker.

A typical service head consists of two 120-volt wires and one neutral wire that deliver power to lights and appliances around the home. The 120-volt circuits use one phase of the electrical service to power standard home appliances.
